THE BELLEVUE MARKET
Buying a Home in Bellevue, WA

Bellevue is the Eastside's flagship city — a gleaming downtown of high-rises, headquarters for major tech employers, and some of the most sought-after neighborhoods in the Seattle region. The median home price is around $1.5 million, and with inventory tight and homes often selling in about a week near full asking, buyers face serious competition.

The top-ranked Bellevue School District draws families from around the world, and the 2 Line light rail now connects Downtown Bellevue to Seattle and the rest of the Eastside. From the luxury estates of West Bellevue and Somerset to the family neighborhoods of Lake Hills, Crossroads, and Factoria, Bellevue spans a wide range of homes — and prices.

When a typical Bellevue purchase runs well over a million dollars, a 2.5% commission is a staggering sum. For a flat $2,999, I write and negotiate a sharp, competitive offer and guide you through closing — so you compete at the top of this market and keep your savings.
