King County & 东区 First-Time Home Buyer Guide
King County & the Eastside

Buying your first home in King County can feel overwhelming — high prices, fast-moving listings, and a stack of unfamiliar paperwork. This guide walks you through it step by step: setting a realistic budget, getting pre-approved, tapping Washington down payment assistance, making a competitive offer, and getting to closing — while keeping more of your money with a flat-fee agent.

在 King County 买第一套房可能让人不知所措 —— 高房价、抢手的房源，以及一堆陌生的文件。本指南带你一步步走完全程：设定务实的预算、获得贷款预批、申请华盛顿州首付援助、写出有竞争力的 Offer、顺利过户 —— 同时用 Flat Fee 经纪人留住更多自己的钱。

第一步：算清你的预算Step 1: Know your real budget

Beyond the price tag, first-time buyers should plan for: a down payment (as little as 3–5% with many programs), closing costs (~2–4% of the price), earnest money (1–3%, applied at closing), and a home inspection (~$400–$600). A good rule: get clear on your monthly comfort zone, not just the maximum a lender approves. The CFPB's Owning a Home tools are a great neutral starting point.

除了房价，首次买家还要预留：首付（许多项目低至 3–5%）、过户费用（约房价的 2–4%）、订金 Earnest Money（1–3%，过户时计入）、以及验房费（约 $400–$600）。一个好原则：弄清你每月真正能负担的舒适区间，而不只是贷款机构批准的上限。CFPB 的 Owning a Home 工具是很好的中立起点。

第二步：贷款预批Step 2: Get pre-approved

A pre-approval letter from a lender shows sellers you're serious and tells you exactly what you can borrow. In a competitive market like the Eastside, you generally shouldn't tour seriously — or write an offer — without one. Shop two or three lenders; rates and fees vary.

来自贷款机构的预批信（Pre-Approval Letter）向卖家表明你是认真的，也让你清楚自己能借多少。在东区这样竞争激烈的市场，没有预批信通常不宜认真看房或写 Offer。多比较两三家贷款机构；利率和费用各有不同。

第三步：华盛顿州首付援助Step 3: Washington down payment assistance

Don't assume you need 20% down. The Washington State Housing Finance Commission (WSHFC) offers down payment assistance and special loan programs for eligible first-time buyers. Ask your lender which WSHFC or other programs you qualify for — it can dramatically lower your upfront cash.

别以为一定要付 20% 首付。华盛顿州住房金融委员会（WSHFC）为符合资格的首次买家提供首付援助和特别贷款项目。问问你的贷款机构你符合哪些 WSHFC 或其他项目 —— 这能大幅降低你的前期现金压力。

第四步：找房 & 选区Step 4: Search smart by neighborhood

King County is huge, and prices vary widely. The Eastside spans premium Bellevue, tech-driven Redmond, and walkable, waterfront Kirkland. Browse Zillow and Redfin daily, attend open houses, and narrow to areas that fit your budget and commute. See local market notes for Redmond, Bellevue, and Kirkland.

King County 很大，价格差异也大。东区涵盖高端的 Bellevue、科技驱动的 Redmond，以及可步行的滨水城市 Kirkland。每天刷 Zillow、Redfin，多参加 Open House，把范围缩小到符合预算与通勤的区域。查看各地市场说明：Redmond、Bellevue、Kirkland。

第五步：写出有竞争力的 OfferStep 5: Make a competitive offer

This is where representation matters most. A strong offer balances price with the right terms — escalation clauses, contingency choices, and clean financing — to win without overpaying. Since the 2024 NAR settlement, you negotiate your agent's fee directly (more on who pays the buyer's agent in Washington).

这一步最能体现代理的价值。一份强有力的 Offer 在价格与条款之间取得平衡 —— Escalation 条款、Contingency 取舍、干净的贷款方案 —— 既能赢得又不至于多付。自 2024 年 NAR 和解后，你直接协商经纪人费用（详见 华盛顿州买方经纪人佣金由谁支付）。

第六步：检查、贷款、过户Step 6: Inspection, loan, and closing

After mutual acceptance: schedule your inspection, your lender orders the appraisal and underwrites the loan, the title company handles escrow, and you do a final walkthrough before signing. Your agent tracks every deadline so no contingency lapses. Then — you get the keys.

在双方接受（Mutual Acceptance）后：安排验房，贷款机构安排估价并审核贷款，Title Company 处理 Escrow，签字前你做最后验房（Final Walkthrough）。你的经纪人盯紧每个截止日期，确保没有 Contingency 过期。然后 —— 你就拿到钥匙了。
