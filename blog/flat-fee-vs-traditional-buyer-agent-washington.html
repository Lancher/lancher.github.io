Flat Fee vs. 传统买方经纪人
你到底要付多少？ Flat Fee vs. Traditional
Buyer's Agent in WA

Shi Hao Liu
华盛顿州持牌房地产经纪人 ・ Kelly Right Real Estate ・ 更新于 2026 年 6 月 21 日 Licensed WA Real Estate Broker ・ Kelly Right Real Estate ・ Updated June 21, 2026

Real estate agent fees in WA aren't fixed — and that matters. A traditional buyer's agent in Washington typically costs about 2.5% of the purchase price — $20,000 on an $800K home, or ~$32,500 on a $1.3M Eastside home. A flat-fee buyer's agent charges one set amount — for example $2,999 — for the core representation. Since the 2024 NAR settlement, you negotiate that fee directly — so the difference stays in your pocket.

在华盛顿州，传统买方经纪人通常收取房价的约 2.5% —— $80 万的房子约 $20,000，$130 万的东区房子约 $32,500。而 Flat Fee（固定收费）买方经纪人只收一笔固定费用 —— 例如 $2,999 —— 完成核心代理工作。自 2024 年 NAR 和解后，这笔费用由你直接协商 —— 差额留在你自己口袋里。

传统买方经纪人怎么收费How traditional buyer's agents charge

Traditionally, the buyer's agent was paid roughly 2.5%–3% of the sale price. It felt "free" because it came out of the sale proceeds — but it was built into the price you paid. The percentage model means the more expensive the home, the more your agent earns, even though the work is largely the same.

传统上，买方经纪人收取成交价的约 2.5%–3%。因为是从成交款里支付，所以感觉「免费」—— 但其实已经计入你支付的房价。按比例收费意味着房子越贵，经纪人赚得越多，尽管工作量大致相同。

Flat Fee 怎么运作How flat fee works

A flat-fee agent charges one transparent price regardless of the home's value. My service is $2,999 and covers the work that actually protects you: writing a competitive offer, reviewing the contract, handling counter-offers, and supporting you through closing. Because you tour and find homes yourself, the fee stays low — and on a million-dollar purchase, that difference is enormous.

Flat Fee 经纪人无论房价高低都收一笔透明的固定费用。我的服务为 $2,999，涵盖真正保护你的工作：撰写有竞争力的 Offer、审查合同、处理还价、协助过户。由于看房、找房由你自己完成，费用才能保持低廉 —— 在百万级的交易中，这个差额非常可观。

哪种更适合你？Which is right for you?

Be honest with yourself about how you shop. A flat fee is ideal if you're comfortable finding and touring homes yourself (most buyers already browse Zillow and Redfin daily) and you want expert help where it counts — the offer, the contract, and closing. A traditional agent may suit you better if you want someone to drive you to many showings and fully manage your search. Both are licensed representation; it comes down to which service — and price — fits how you buy.

先诚实评估自己的买房方式。如果你愿意自己找房、看房（大多数买家本来就每天刷 Zillow、Redfin），并希望在关键环节 —— Offer、合同、过户 —— 获得专业协助，那么 Flat Fee 非常适合你。如果你希望有人带你看很多套房、全程代为搜寻，传统经纪人可能更合适。两者都是持牌代理；关键在于哪种服务与价格更符合你的买房方式。

结论The bottom line

Since you now negotiate buyer-agent compensation directly, paying a percentage is a choice — not a requirement. If you're a capable, motivated buyer in Seattle or the Eastside, a flat $2,999 gets you the expert representation that matters while keeping tens of thousands of dollars at closing.
