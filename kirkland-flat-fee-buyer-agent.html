THE KIRKLAND MARKET
Buying a Home in Kirkland, WA

Kirkland is one of the Eastside's most beloved cities — a walkable, waterfront downtown on Lake Washington, with marinas, parks, and a lively restaurant and gallery scene. The median home price is around $1.4 million, and proximity to the lake and the charming downtown core keeps demand high and inventory low.

Major employers like Google at Kirkland Urban anchor the local economy, and the highly rated Lake Washington School District serves families across neighborhoods like Juanita, Houghton, Rose Hill, Finn Hill, and the redeveloped Totem Lake. Whether you want a waterfront condo downtown or a family home on the hill, Kirkland rewards a fast, well-prepared offer.

On a typical Kirkland home, a 2.5% commission runs $35,000 or more. For a flat $2,999, I handle the offer, negotiation, and closing — so you move quickly in a tight market and keep tens of thousands at closing.
