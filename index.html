签署服务协议后支付，透明公开，没有任何隐藏费用。

什么是 Buyer Brokerage Agreement？+

这是 Washington 州法律（RCW 18.86）要求的书面服务协议。在我们开始为你提供服务之前必须签署。协议预设期限为 60 天，内容包括服务范围、费用（$2,999 flat fee）、以及代理关系类型。你可以选择 exclusive 或 nonexclusive。

Do you show homes?+

Our service focuses on offer writing and contract handling. You find the home yourself — that's how we keep our fee low. However, we will be present at the Final Walkthrough to help with access and ensure everything checks out.

How many offers are included?+

$2,999 includes up to 3 offer submissions, plus full support from offer to closing, including counter-offer negotiations.

How much are real estate agent fees in WA?+

Traditionally, a buyer's agent in Washington charged about 2.5% of the purchase price — $20,000+ on an $800K home. As a flat fee buyer's agent serving King County and the Eastside, I charge one flat $2,999 for the same core representation, so your real estate agent fees stay low and you keep the difference.

Are you licensed?+

Yes, I'm a licensed Real Estate Broker in Washington State (License #26003789) with Kelly Right Real Estate.

When do I pay?+

Payment is due after signing the service agreement. Transparent pricing, no hidden fees.

What is a Buyer Brokerage Agreement?+

It's a written service agreement required by Washington State law (RCW 18.86) before we begin providing services. The default term is 60 days. It outlines our services, fee ($2,999 flat fee), and agency relationship. You can choose exclusive or nonexclusive representation.
